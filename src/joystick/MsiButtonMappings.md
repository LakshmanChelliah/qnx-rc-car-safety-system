# MSI USB Dongle Controller

## Analog Mode Button Mapping
Analog mode works with QNX
You know its in analog mode when the first and third leds are lit on the front of the joystick

X: Button 0
A: Button 1
B: Button 2
Y: Button 3
LB: Button 4
RB: Button 5
LT: Button 6
RT: Button 7
Back: Button 8
Start: Button 9
Left Stick Down: Button 10
Right Stick Down: Button 11

D-pad up: Axis 5 goes to -32767
D-pad down: Axis 5 goes to 32767
D-pad left: Axis 4 goes to -32767
D-pad right: Axis 4 goes to 32767

Left Stick Horizontal Axis: Axis 0 -32767 to 32767
Left Stick Vertical Axis: Axis 1 -32767 to 32767
Right Stick Horizontal Axis:  Axis 2 -32767 to 32767
Right Stick Vertical Axis:  Axis 3 -32767 to 32767
